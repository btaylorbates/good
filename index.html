Twist Society looks after all your taxation and accounting needs, either in person, via email or over the phone. Whether its personal or business taxes, accounting, software or bookkeeping services – it’s what we do!

Welcome

Twist Society provides friendly, professional accounting and tax services – we make it easy for you! We don’t believe your accountant should just be a form filler. We will provide you with the support network you need to take the financial stress out of running your own business.

ACCOUNTING

We offer accounting and bookkeeping services, as well as payroll support for small and medium businesses. Our clients are diverse in size from small sole traders to medium size companies that are involved in a variety of different industries.

Whether you are starting up a new business or need help with your existing business, we can advise you in areas that are relevant to your situation.

TAX

Twist Society can simplify all your tax needs from preparation to submission – no more forms to fill in! We cover all areas of taxation from simple individual tax returns to business tax obligations. This includes all taxes for companies, trusts, partnerships and self managed super funds.

We are experienced with dealing with the ATO and can liaise with them on your behalf regarding lodgement and debt arrangements.

ABOUT US

At Twist Society we want to make your accounting needs and tax compliance easy so you spend more time doing what you do best – grow your business! We make it simple and easy with today’s technologies. Let us help you and your business SUCCEED!

Essential Accounting & Tax Services is based in Kellyville Ridge. Every client deals directly with our principal Cathy Jones who has been in the public accounting industry for more than 20 years.

Cathy Jones has a Bachelor of Commerce, is a member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants Australia & New Zealand and also a Registered Tax Agent with the Australian Taxation Office.
